Company Profile

Since its inception in 1978, Karlin Pharmaceuticals and Exports Private Limited has been competently led by Mr. Allaudin Ahamed as the Managing Director.

A pioneer in the category of wide derma formulations in India, Karlin Pharmaceuticals and Exports Private Limited is one of the leader in Derma segment.

The company’s growth is a result of its unwavering commitment to manufacturing and supplying quality products, ethical promotion, and its strong sales and marketing prowess, all of which have earned it the continued patronage of the medical fraternity and growing market acceptance.

With about 30 years of history, the company has grown from humble beginnings to become a formidable organization, with state-of-the-art manufacturing capabilities, a nation-wide market presence, a robust distribution network.
