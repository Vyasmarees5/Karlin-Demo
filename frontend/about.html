About Us

VISION

To be the most trusted leader in Derma healthcare, valued by doctors, patients, pharmacists, distributors, employees, investors, and the communities we serve.

MISSION

To innovate, manufacture, and market world-class dermatology products that prevent and cure skin diseases, improving the quality of life for millions.

As a recognized leader in dermatology marketing and manufacturing, we aim to be a one stop partner for derma brands worldwide.
Through our Contract Manufacturing Organization (CMO) / Party to Party (P2P) /Third Party Manufacturing services, we enable our partners to focus on brand building while we ensure high-quality, cost-efficient, and on-time production.

Company Profile

Established in 1997, Karlin Pharmaceuticals is a pioneer in dermatology formulations in India. Under the visionary leadership of Mr. B. Allauddin Ahamed, the company has grown from a modest unit into a nationwide and export-ready organization.

Our success is powered by state-of-the-art GMP / GLP / ISO-certified facilities, strong R&D, and a robust marketing and distribution network.
